Target Audience

The target audience for this website are for those who are looking to get an update on upcoming rain and other precipitation related weathers. This site if for those who love to do things in the rain, or simply for those who are looking to have a look in the future about the rain.

Personas

  • Name: Clark. Age 30. Likes gardening, community service, and volunteering at shelters. Storm chaser on the side.
  • Name: Lois. Age 29. Likes gardening, lives in an apartment, urban explorer. Likes to have cookie parties when it's the raining.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: A man that lives in the city wants to relax on the rain, but is wanting to know when the perfect time to clock off is
  • Scenario 2: A mother wants to have her me time at her she-shed, but is wanting to know if 5:00 PM would be just cloudy, or if it's going to rain
  • Scenario 3: A gardening hobbyist is wondering when rain is coming so that the freshly planted trees can be showered with natural rain
  • Scneario 4: A couple wants to play in the rain as a non-traditional date idea, and they want to know when it will rain
  • Scenario 5: A girl is planning on walking her dog, but wants to know if it will be a hassle to walk the dog because she heard rumors that it will rain at the time she planned on
