The purpose for this site is to provide people an insight to the weather that is happening around the area. This way, people can predict if rain will come or not— hence the name "Petrichor", which is derived from a word that means "a pleasant smell that frequently accompanies the first rain after a long period of warm, dry weather." —Oxford Dictionary
